About ETOS

ETOS will be resuming activity with multiple TOs operating alongside each other to ensure a higher frequency and quality of tournaments. The goal is to turn ETOS into the EU version of COSCORD / BestieInSlot. We want it to be the hub for European tournaments so that everything can be easily found in one place. NA are a perfect example of this done correctly and we hope to replicate their results with their help. Additionally, we now have two new TOs: Lylaeth & zoe.

Tournaments

The ETOS team brings you the first bi-weekly tournament from our kitchen. The first Floppa Cup is landing, giving y’all something to play for in the spirit of the ol’ Monkey Cup. Primarily a for fun tournament to put teams against each other in the core striking ring. Floppa is meant to be a community vision, however for the first ever Floppa we will be contributing a small 30€ prize pool for the winners of the event.

Visit start.gg to signup or find the rules here.
