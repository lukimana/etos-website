About ETOS

ETOS plans to be the hub for competetive Omega Strikers in Europe. We’ve got multiple TOs operating alongside each other to ensure a higher frequency and quality of tournaments. We are following the footsteps of Coscord and BestieInSlot. The North American region is a perfect example of this done correctly and we hope to replicate those results with their help.

Tournaments

There will be regular bi-weekly tournaments. Those are meant to be a regular staple, that are meant to be a fun, yet still a competetive way to experience Omega Strikers at it’s full potenial. All streamed live on Twitch!

Of course there are plans for serious and competetive tournaments, with a set prizepool! We might also do the occasional one-off with a special ruleset like The Starcade
